<p>In Microsoft Defender for Office 365, we are updating the way end users allow and block emails in Exchange Online.&nbsp;<br></p><p>This message is associated with Microsoft 365 Roadmap ID <a href="https://www.microsoft.com/microsoft-365/roadmap?filters=&amp;searchterms=380185" target="_blank">380185</a>.</p><p><b>When this will happen:</b></p><p>Standard Release, GCC, and GCC High: We will begin rolling out mid-Feb 2024 and expect to complete by mid-March 2024.</p><p>DoD: We will begin rolling out mid-March 2024 and expect to complete by mid-April 2024.</p><p><b>How this will affect your organization:</b><br></p><p>With one click, end users will block emails from unwanted senders and prevent those emails from showing up in their default quarantine view and in their quarantine notifications.
