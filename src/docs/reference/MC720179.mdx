<p>With Collaborative Stageview, Microsoft Teams enables you to open your app content in new multi-window experiences. Configure your multi-window to include an associated conversation or&nbsp;enable a focused view for your user to complete their task. Collaborative Stageview can be opened from an adaptive card, Teams JS SDK or deeplink.
